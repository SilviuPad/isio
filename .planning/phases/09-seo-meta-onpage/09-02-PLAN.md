<objective>
Expand all 5 service page meta descriptions from ~34 characters to 120-160 characters in both Romanian and English, with unique keyword-rich content per service.

Purpose: Thin meta descriptions (~34 chars) hurt CTR in SERPs. Google truncates at ~160 chars and may ignore descriptions under ~70 chars entirely. Expanding to 120-160 chars with relevant keywords improves both indexing and click-through rate (audit #17).
Output: 5 updated JSON files in src/content/services/ with expanded metaDescription fields.
</objective>
